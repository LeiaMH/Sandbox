Quidditch

Hogwarts is proud to have four excellent quidditch teams, one for each house. Team try outs are usually held within the first month of the new term. We also offer quidditch lessons for those wishing to learn how to play. Madam Hooch has plenty of experience. She has taught students who have gone on to play professionally.

The Four Houses

At the beginning of first year all students are sorted into one of our four houses which have been named after our founding fathers Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. Each has its own common room, dormitories, and quidditch team. The houses compete throughout the school year, by earning and losing points for behaviour. At the end of the year the house with the most points wins the House Cup.

Hogwarts Grounds

The grounds offer a beautiful setting for students to relax in. At the foot of the castle sits the school lake, not far from the forest, home to many magical creatures which are being looked after by our grounds keeper Rubeus Hagrid. Students, however, are prohibited from entering the forest without supervision.

Visit Hogsmead

Students in third year and above are invited to visit the local village on weekends, granted their partents give them permission. Taste Madam Rosmerta's delicious butter beer at The Three Broomsticks, or pay a visit to the Shrieking Shack, a haunted house on the outskirts of the village.
